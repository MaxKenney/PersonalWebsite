PacMan VR

Relive the classic arcade title ‘PacMan’ in Virtual Reality, in this game you must collect all the pellets scattered around the maze before the ghosts find and kill you. If you feel they are beginning to surround you, run for the super fruits which terrify the ghosts and make them vulnerable for a short time!

The 4 different ghosts all use the intelligent hunting algorithms used in the original games to surround you as well as occasionally enter the scatter state from the original game to spread out.

Additionally as you progress through the levels, the ghosts will rest less and be less afraid of the super fruit so the challenge increases.

City Experience VR

Experience the sensations of walking around a concrete jungle with busy traffic and pedestrians moving through the crowd.

Focusing on immersion, sound and weather have been implemented in addition to a day night cycle with a world that reacts to the time of day.

Featuring a complex traffic system that is capable of moving cars realistically through a T-junction.
